Our Mission

Our goal is to spread awareness on puppy mills and educate the public on how to identify a breeder as a puppy mill. BreederBook will also be used as a means of uncovering existing puppy mills across America.

How it Works

While rating a breeder, you will be asked to answer a couple of questions about your purchase. These questions will act as indicators as to wether or not the breeder is a potential puppy mill.
